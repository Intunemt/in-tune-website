How Does Music Therapy Work?

Music therapy Most are very curious when they learn about music therapy about “how it works”. Do I have to be a musician? Do we just play music? When do I talk about my problems? Music Therapy has a very concise definition according to the American Music Therapy Association. It mentions a therapeutic relationship, clinical/evidenced base use of music, credentialed professionals, goals… its a lot. What does it boil down to? I use music to achieve Non musical goals. Musical goals would be things like learning how to play an instrument, the difference between augmented sixth chords, what effect Beethoven's deafness had on his contribution to romantic music… All fine and dandy, but non musical goals like expressing emotion safely, understanding patterns of behavior, reducing symptoms of depression/anxiety etc, navigating trauma… those are what drives it all for me as a music therapist. Best of all, you don't need to even be able to carry a tune in a bucket! My training has prepared me for this.
