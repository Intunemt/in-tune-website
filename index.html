Meet Makenzie

Board Certified Music Therapist

I bring warmth, intuition, and creativity to every session. With over 15 years of experience and a foundation in Dialectical Behavior Therapy, I’ve supported clients through a wide range of mental health challenges. I provide affirming, compassionate, and authentic care for neurodivergent and LGBTQIA+ individuals — with humor, directness, and heart.

Why Music Therapy?

Embody Your Authentic Self

Music therapy provides a space transcendent to words to explore emotion, identity, and the self, especially when the truth is beyond words.

Regulate Emotions

Co-create safety through rhythm and tone.

Increase Self-Awareness

Sound reveals what words often conceal.

Strengthen Relationships

Explore connection through shared musical experience.

Process Trauma

Access non-verbal pathways to heal safely.

Reduce Stress

This isn't about just listening to a relaxation track. It's about learning how to use your preferred music or creating to get your stress level to where you want it to be.

Increase Focus

Music activates presence and cognitive clarity.
