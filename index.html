Meet Makenzie

Board Certified Music Therapist

I bring warmth, validation, and creativity to every session. With over 15 years of experience and a foundation in Dialectical Behavior Therapy, I've used Music Therapy to support clients through a wide range of mental health challenges. I provide affirming, compassionate, and authentic care with humor, directness, curiosity, and heart.

I am an ally, anti-diet, as well as ENM and neurodivergence affirming.

Our Mission

To provide evidence based music therapy that empowers individuals to become in tune with their authentic self by cultivating personal growth and meaningful change. Individuals can expect the specialized use of music to provide a unique, holistic and transformative healing experience that oozes compassion, affirmation, safety and acceptance.

Our Vision

To redefine the meaning of mental health care by affirming music therapy as a transformative approach beyond talk therapy. We envision a future where creative arts therapies are universally recognized for their effective power in healing, self-discovery and personal growth.

Why Music Therapy?

Embody Your Authentic Self

Music therapy provides a space transcendent to words to explore emotion, identity, and the self, especially when the truth is beyond words.

Regulate Emotions

The path of music through the brain involves the emotion center twice. Music deeply affects emotions.

Increase Self-Awareness

Learn more about your authentic self through music therapy experiences.

Strengthen Relationships

Explore connection with yourself or others through the therapeutic process.

Process Trauma

Access non-verbal pathways to heal safely.

Reduce Stress

This isn't about just listening to a relaxation track. Use your music to get your stress level to where you want it to be.

Increase Focus

Music activates presence and cognitive clarity.
